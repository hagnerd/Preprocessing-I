INTRODUCTION

My journey into programming really started when I wanted to make a killer MySpace page for my hardcore band. I saw that my highschool offered an introduction to web development course and I signed up. My high school band didn't last for long, but my interest in programming haunted me all throughout college and into my proffesional life. I found myself trying wedge programming in wherever I could.

One day, while writing a printer utility in AppleScripts I realized I had messed up. I was meant to be a programmer. I started learning as much as I could. After a year of going the self taught route I heard about Lambda, and it was too good of an offer to pass up.
